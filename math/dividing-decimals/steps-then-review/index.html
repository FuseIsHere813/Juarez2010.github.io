Before we start, let's review some basic division vocabulary.


Divisor: The number outside the equation.


Dividend: The number inside the equation.


Quotient: The answer to a division problem. "root"

These are the steps on how to do it.


Step 1: Make the divisor a whole number by moving the decimal to the right.


Step 2: "Monkey see, monkey do."

Move the dividend decimal to match.


Step 3: Move the decimal to the roof straight up from the dividend.


Step 4: Divide as usual.
